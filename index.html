MACEHUB

The hub of all factions at MACE

Connected

Macehub connects each and every department an clubs of the campus and provides them an online platform to manage the events.

Hassle Free

Ever had trouble organising events without clashes with others, well Macehub solves this as now each and every club can know of each other's events.

Service

We provide blogs for the clubs and departments so that they can tell their stories to the whole world, without having to worry of the technical side.
